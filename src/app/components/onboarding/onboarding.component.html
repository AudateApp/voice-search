Step 1: Pin the extension

The browser icon is the only way of interacting with this extension, pinning it makes it accessible.

  1. Click on the extensions icon.
  2. Find Audate in the list of extensions.
  3. Click on the pin icon on the right of Audate icon.

Step 2: Grant microphone permission

This permission is necessary to record audio and convert it to search query.
